
# Phandalin

Phandalin is a small rough-and-tumble frontier town. Rumors of a new gold mine and potential treasure in the ruins around it attracting many adventurers. The town has potential to grow and flourish. But with Mount Hotenow erupting and destroying neighboring settlements, monster attacks increasing, and bandits harassing locals--there's also the potential for it to be abandoned yet again.
