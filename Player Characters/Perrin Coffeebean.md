# Perrin Coffeebean

A spry 72 year old halfling who is busy getting his Anthropology Masters degree in ancient dwarven settlements. He spent most of his meagre funding to get to [Phandalin](../Sword%20Coast/Phandalin.md) first before his clan rival, [Appleyn Tealeaf](../Characters/Appelyn%20Tealeaf.md), and now finds himself in "a transactional yet mutually beneficial exchange." 

His time immersed in dwarven culture has allowed him to discover a new side of him (barbarian). In an ideal world people would talk things out like civilized folk, but Perrin is not too naïve to be caught in idealism, and won't shy away from violence when it comes down to it. Sometimes the cure to hard heads is a harder fist.

## Companions

### Duncan Wheatring

Perrin and [Duncan](../Characters/Duncan%20Wheatring.md) met in a tavern. Perrin drinking to celebrate finding a new lead in Phandalin while Duncan was drinking to drown his sorrows on his lost dream of becoming a knight. Perrin convinced him that it's never too late to follow his dream of becoming a knight and offered him a job in Phandalin to kickstart his journey.

Perrin remembers none of this the next morning but doesn't want to hurt his feelings, so he pulls some strings and sends a letter to Duncan's parents that he'll take good care of him.

### Gundren Rockseeker

Gundren is the one who gave Perrin a new lead to his research, and asked him to join the expedition in finding the [Forge of Spells](../Sword%20Coast/Forge%20of%20Spells.md). He also helped Perrin get Duncan a mentor, [Sildar Hallwinter](../Characters/Sildar%20Hallwinter).
